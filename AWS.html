November 2015

My Experience !!

Providing more details on my experience, I worked at Oracle India for a year as a QA Automation Intern, designing and developing automation scripts and testing Oracle Retail products.

For the past one year, I have been working as Graduate Teaching Assistant at Illinois Institute of Technology. My job responsibilities include assisting students to learn technologies for Modeling, Designing and Developing Software Applications using Object Oriented Languages, organizing Web Development Projects, deliver lectures every week and managing a class of over 120 students.

Since joining Illinois Institute of Technology, I have worked on challenging projects that are out of my comfort zone and learned a lot of new technical skills.
